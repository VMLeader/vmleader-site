Ongoing Services

It's lonely at the very top.

Every hard call, every big bet, every private doubt eventually lands on one desk. Vertical Motion partners with you as a strategic advisor to the CEO, so you have a trusted, outside voice in the room for the decisions that matter most.

Talk to us →

Why it matters

The isolation is real, and so is the cost of going it alone.

61% of CEOs who feel lonely in the role say it hinders their performance. Half of CEOs report feeling lonely at all. Harvard Business Review / RHR International
~2 in 3 CEOs receive no coaching or leadership advice from an outside advisor, yet nearly all of them say they want it. Stanford GSB / The Miles Group
529% return on investment reported in a widely cited Fortune 500 study of executive coaching, up to 788% counting retention. MetrixGlobal (Merrill C. Anderson)

Figures are drawn from published research and are directional. A trusted advisor won't fix everything, but going without one has a real and measurable cost.

What a strategic advisor gives you

One trusted voice, outside the org chart.

Not a consultant with a deliverable and not a direct report with a stake in the answer. A partner whose only agenda is you and the company doing well.

A confidential sounding board

Think out loud with someone who has no politics in your company and nothing to protect. Say the quiet part and work it through.

Outside perspective

Pattern-matched across many CEOs and companies, not just your own history, so you see around corners you can't see from inside.

Big decisions, stress-tested

Pressure-test the bet before you commit capital, headcount, or reputation to it. Better to find the flaw in a session than in the market.

Real accountability

Someone who remembers what you said you'd do, asks about it, and holds you to the standard you set for yourself.

Board-ready thinking

Get sharp before the boardroom, not during it. Walk in with your narrative, your numbers, and your asks already pressure-tested.

Steadier under pressure

A place to process the hard stuff so it doesn't leak into the team. Your composure sets the emotional weather for the whole company.

How we work with you

On-call counsel, plus a steady rhythm.

You get a regular cadence of working sessions with a principal advisor, and a line you can pick up between them when a real decision lands. We're in your corner for the big moments, not just scheduled around them.

Every engagement is private and built around you, the CEO. Where it helps, we bring in the rest of the ELT Scaling Suite, but the advisory relationship is yours.

Where it fits

The CEO's own layer of the ELT Scaling Suite.

While the leadership team gets coaching and the business gets diagnosed, the CEO gets a dedicated advisor. It's the difference between carrying it all alone and having one person you can be completely straight with.
