Why most people fail

Don't fall into the "Wantrepreneur" trap.

Most people who dream of leaving corporate start with the busy work: a website, a logo, a brand, a product built over months. By the time they finally talk to a buyer, they are out of runway, confused, and frustrated.

The Revenue First Framework™ flips the order. You validate income first and skip everything that doesn't lead to a paying client. Real sales conversations come early, not last.
