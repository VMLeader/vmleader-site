The fine print

How the credit works.

Two ways to earn: introduce someone who takes a strategy call, or refer someone who enrolls in a flagship program. Here is how each credit works.

  • Strategy call tier: when someone you introduce completes a strategy call, we credit one month ($197) toward your Community membership. There is no limit on how many months you can earn, and each person who takes a call earns another.
  • The person you introduce receives the free LDNA assessment and a personalized debrief on their call, whether or not they enroll in anything.
  • Membership credit posts after the strategy call takes place and applies to the $197 Community tier.
  • Enrollment tier: your friend receives 7.5% off enrollment, and you receive a matching 7.5% as account credit.
  • Credit posts after your friend enrolls and their payment clears. It holds for 30 days, then becomes available to use.
  • Credit is good toward our programs, your monthly membership, courses, or events. It expires 12 months from issue, is non-transferable, and has no cash value.
  • One referral benefit applies per enrollment.
  • This referral program is only eligible for our coaching programs, not courses or trainings.
