Questions

What you are probably wondering.

How is Elevate different from the Executive Career Accelerator (ECA)?

The ECA is our high-touch, six-month implementation program with group coaching and unlimited 1:1 support. Elevate is a membership that gives you the same course material, live trainings, and the Vertical Motion community in a more self-guided way, for people who are not ready for a $10K investment.

Do I get access to the same curriculum?

Yes. You get the Whole Executive Course, the same material covered in the ECA. You can also add some products and mini-courses included in the ECA, such as the Foundations Report.

How much time does this take each week?

As little or as much as you want. The course is on-demand and self-paced, so you move at your own speed. The one live anchor is the weekly Leadership Edge call, around 60 to 90 minutes, and every session is recorded if you cannot attend live. Most members get real traction with a few focused hours a week, even with a demanding schedule.

Is the membership month-to-month?

Yes. Join anytime, cancel anytime. You pay for a full month at a time, so if you cancel you keep access until the end of your billing cycle.

Who are the weekly Leadership Edge calls for?

The entire Vertical Motion community: 1:1 clients, alumni, and Elevate members come together to discuss leadership, growth, and key executive skills. Calls are recorded and available on the community portal and apps.

Is this for managers, or only directors?

Elevate is for anyone with at least 10 years of corporate experience who has managed people at least once and has a goal to reach the executive level. It works for senior managers, directors, and some high-level individual contributors.

What if it is not the right fit?

Give it a fair shot: attend at least two Leadership Edge calls and complete onboarding. If it is not for you, let us know within 30 days and we will cancel and refund your first month.
