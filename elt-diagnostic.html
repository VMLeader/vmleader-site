ELT Diagnostic

The ELT Advanced Assessment. A three to four week deep read of your executive leadership team: how it's composed, where it's exposed, and exactly what to fix first, delivered as a risk map and a 90-day plan.

What's included

A complete read of your leadership team, in three to four weeks.

Built from every seat on the team, not just the view from the corner office.

1

Assessments and 1:1 interviews

Leadership team assessments plus a one-on-one interview with each member of the ELT, so the picture is built from every seat.

2

Half-day alignment workshop

A half-day workshop detailing team blind spots and overlap risks, to align on org design and operating system.

3

Risk Map, CEO debrief, and 90-day plan

A Leadership Team Risk Map with your top risks, plus a private 1:1 CEO debrief and a 90-day plan for the CEO.

Who it's built for

Not for every CEO. The right fit matters.

The ELT Scaling Suite is designed for a specific kind of leader at a specific kind of inflection point.

Scaling CEOs at $5M to $50M

You've got product-market fit. The team that got you here isn't necessarily the team that gets you there.

Pre-raise or pre-transition CEOs

Investors scrutinize the team first. Know your exposure before they do.

CEOs who feel like the bottleneck

When every hard call lands on your desk, that's not a strategy problem. It's a team architecture problem.
