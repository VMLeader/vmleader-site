The real bottleneck

Your ELT is either your biggest growth lever – or your biggest growth constraint.

Aligned, healthy executive teams are 2x as likely to hit financial targets (McKinsey), and their companies are 6x more likely to be revenue & profit leaders in their industry (Bain & Co.).

But most C-suites and ELT just don't have "it". 65% of CEOs describe their ELTs as ineffective (Bain & Co.) Worse, only 31% of ELT members think of the C-suite as their primary team, preferring to stay siloed in their function vs. thinking about the enterprise.

When the ELT works, the business wins. When it doesn't…performance tanks and problems spiral out of control, leaving the CEO to do the heavy lifting and referee infighting.

We show you how to see, measure, and fix these problems before they become expensive failures. We help put the right leaders in the right roles, with the right support, across the entire value stream.
