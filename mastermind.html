Executive Mastermind Coaching

Exclusive 1:1 coaching for C-suite members.

A private, fully customized engagement with Tom Cox, offered by invitation only. Tom partners with a small number of CEOs and C-level leaders over 6 to 12 months, built entirely around your priorities, your team, and the decisions only you can make.

Request an Invitation → By application · limited spots
By invitation only. A handful of CEOs and C-suite leaders at a time.
What this is

A private coaching engagement, built entirely around you.

Tom works 1:1 with a small number of CEOs and C-suite leaders at a time. There is no fixed curriculum and no cohort. Every engagement is shaped around the decisions, people, and pressures specific to your seat.

1

Fully customized to you

We start from your priorities, not a template. The work flexes as your business and your role evolve.

2

A 6 to 12 month engagement

A long enough runway to do real work on what actually moves the needle, not a quick-fix sprint.

3

Direct 1:1 access to Tom

You work with Tom himself, with regular sessions plus support between them when the stakes are high.

4

By invitation only

Tom takes on only a handful of leaders at a time so each engagement gets real depth and attention.

Who Tom works with

Built for leaders carrying the weight at the top.

This engagement is a fit if you are a CEO or C-level leader who wants a trusted, senior thinking partner in your corner. To keep the work deep and personal, places are limited and offered by invitation.

  • ▸ You are a CEO or C-suite executive making high-stakes calls with few true peers to think them through.
  • ▸ You want coaching shaped around your real agenda, not a packaged program.
  • ▸ You are scaling, navigating a transition, or carrying complexity that nobody else fully sees.
  • ▸ You value a long-term partner who knows your context, not a one-off session.
