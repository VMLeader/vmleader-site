Executive Outplacement

Help your senior leaders land their next role.

Senior-level outplacement built on the Whole Executive Framework™. Give your departing directors, VPs, and executives a dignified exit and a real head start on what comes next.

Talk to Our Team →

The hardest call you make

A senior departure deserves more than a severance letter.

Letting go of a senior manager, director, or executive is one of the hardest decisions a company makes. The business impact is real, and so is the human one. The people who helped build what you have deserve a considered exit and a genuine head start on what comes next.

Vertical Motion provides outplacement built for senior manager level and above. We help your departing leaders move on with clarity and confidence, and we protect the reputation you have spent years earning as an employer people respect.

How it works

Outplacement built on the Whole Executive Framework™.

Every engagement runs on the same system we use to coach sitting executives, not a generic resume workshop.

Your departing leaders get a structured read on how they lead, where they create the most value, and which roles genuinely fit. That clarity is the difference between chasing the first available opening and targeting the right next move. It is also what shortens the search and protects their confidence through a difficult transition.

On-Demand Career Coaching

Senior-level coaching, available when your leaders need it.

One-on-one coaching with senior-level career coaches: positioning, search strategy, interview preparation, and offer negotiation, all tailored to director, VP, and C-suite roles. Your leaders reach for support at the moment a decision is in front of them, not on a fixed schedule that ignores how real searches unfold.

The business case

Senior-level outplacement is the smart move, not just the kind one.

Supporting the leaders you transition protects the company you are still running.

Reduce legal and reputational risk

Demonstrate genuine goodwill at exit and lower the odds of a costly, public dispute.

Protect valuable relationships

The leaders you transition may become clients, partners, or boomerang hires. Leave on terms that keep the door open.

Reassure the leaders who stay

Your remaining team is watching how you treat their peers. A dignified exit protects morale and trust.

Borrow our credibility

Align your brand with a recognized executive coaching partner whose reputation reflects well on you.
